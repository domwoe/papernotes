title={A Large-Scale Analysis of the Security of Embedded Firmwares}
author={Andrei Costin and Jonas Zaddach and Aurelien Francillion and Davide Balzarotti}
year={2014}
keyword={embedded, security}

Statistical analysis of 32 000 firmware images. Found 38 previously unknown vulnerabilites in 693 firmware images corresponding to 123 products with at least 140 000 instances accessible over the Internet.

What the found:
* backdoors
* SSL keypairs
